WxCC Widgets Chat Client Sample

Welcome to the chat client page. This widget allows you to create a chat task for agents on ccsdk.wbx.ai sandbox. Click on the floating icon at the bottom right.

Social: You can also initiate a social chat task by logging in to your Facebook account and sending a message to this Facebook page.
Email: You can initiate an email task by sending an email to ccsdk.wbx.ai@gmail.com.
Where to find your tasks: You can view incoming tasks either in Webex Contact Center Agent Desktop or the WxCC Widget Sample.
